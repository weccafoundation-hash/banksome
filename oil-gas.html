Who We Are

Banksome Oil & Gas is the energy services unit of Banksome Global Resources Ltd, established in 2012 to provide specialized and integrated solutions across the oil and gas value chain.

We combine international best practices with strong local expertise to deliver safe, efficient, and technically sound operations across engineering, logistics, drilling, and production support.
